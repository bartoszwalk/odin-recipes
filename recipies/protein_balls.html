Chocolate Peanut Butter Protein Balls

This is a simple and easy no bake recipie to be used as a quick snack whenever you need that extra protein boost!

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Peanut Butter
  • Honey
  • Protein Powder
  • Chocholate Chips

Method

  1. Mix oats, peanut butter, protein powder and chocolate chips in a large bowl
  2. Knead the dough near the end with your hands
  3. Use a small cookie scoop to scoop and form the dough into balls
  4. Store in a covered container in the fridge or freezer
