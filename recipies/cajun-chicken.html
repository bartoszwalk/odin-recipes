Cajun Chicken Pasta

This pasta is a quick, easy and healthy meal to get your protein and veggies in for the day. Made with simple ingredients to save you time at the super market

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • red onion
  • mushrooms
  • red pepper
  • chicken breast
  • cajun seasonig
  • pasta
  • chicken stock
  • light cream cheese

Method

  1. Heat oil in pan. Add red onion, vegetabes and pepper and cook for a few minutes until soft. Remove from the pan afterwards.
  2. Add a little more olive oil and cook chicken until brown. Add vegetables and Cajun seasoning to tatse.
  3. Add pasta and chicken stock. Cover and simmer for 15 mins.
  4. Stir in cream cheese to make a thick creamy sauce. Servce immediately and enjoy!
